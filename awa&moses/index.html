Our Story

We Come to Love not by finding a perfect person, but by learning to see an imperfect person perfectly

  • First We Met

    We first met 2010 during Grace's birthday party and after the party I couldn't hide my feelings. I decide to shoot my shot but was bounced back so hard that I lost my peace, my sleep and everything about me changed. I knew it was Love and Mehn!!! I was so in love. She finally accepted and then came the day I had to move to Lagos and we both didn't like it but it was necessary, that faithful day I saw a fashion Ring on Awa's finger and I took it and held unto it while I was away till when I came back.

  • The Walk!!!

    Moses: While in Lagos, we talked regularly checking up and building our Love and Trust. Through the good and bad, I can remember a day I came to greet Awa at home in Maiduguri and she didn't allow me enter the house, LOL... We both had our flaws but yet we found a way to accomodate each other and build block on block to where we are today.
    Awa: Moses is amazing, he is patient and kind. There are times I felt so down and he was able to pull me up. But through it all its been an Awesome ride all the way.

  • Engagement

    January 1, 2016

    The first time I proposed to Awa, it didn't go as I pictured in my head. I didn't expect a rejection at all. But that was it. Lol. Its so funny how we sit back and talk of our past stories and Laugh over them. To start with, It took me three years to get an acceptance and that was a day that will take a long while to forget. Our Love started on a new dimension!!! I never for once gave up on her all this years. She's a woman I love and I am always encouraged and motivated by her, She brings out the best in me.
