Get to know about our company

BROMPTON SOLICITORS was established in 2019 by Ekwy Ogbu. The Firm has developed an enviable portfolio of clients and broad experience in National and International legal services and transactions. The Firm’s experience spans commercial and financial transactions, corporate legal services, privatization, mining, real estate, constitutional law, oil and gas and company secretarial services.

BROMPTON SOLICITORS maintains a high level of efficiency, professionalism and confidentiality, positioning itself envisions itself as a frontline law firm in Nigeria with International focus and experience. Its service delivery model empowers the client by keen focus on client’s needs and concerns. Our Firm/client relationship management strategy emphasizes client satisfaction in a timely and efficient manner.
