General Counsel

Brompton Solicitors brings a wide range of knowledge and experience representing clients in litigation in very complex disputes. We understand that sometimes, businesses require more than mere legal representation, and we deliver legal counsel to our clients for strategic business growth and development.

We work with experts across diverse disciplines to give our clients the best services. As such, we are recognized by our clients as competent and efficient, because we ensure our clients receive the best advice they require from experts, both within and outside the firm.

Crisis Management

Companies doing business across Africa are likely to run into crisis, and how a company first responds to a crisis often determines how the discussion goes.

At Brompton Solicitors, our Crisis Management & Prevention Practice is anchored by seasoned lawyers with broad and deep experience working with clients across many industry sectors across Africa. We develop crisis management plans tailored to each client's needs. We assist a wide range of clients in conducting risk assessments as a critical step to reduce the potential for crises. When a crisis hits, we are ready to assist with solutions and next steps.

Financial Services Law & Regulations

To be a great financial advisor, you need to do more than manage client’s finances. You need to be great at managing relationships. Relationships are the lifeblood of an effective financial advisory practice.

Our Financial Advice team at Brompton Solicitors has significant experience advising clients in commercial transactions. We agree that being good with people is what converts prospects to clients, and makes clients feel comfortable enough to refer their friends, family, and colleagues to you.

Our highly-trained team is committed to offering tailor-made financial advice to each of our clients.

StartUps

The world is a fast-paced environment, and the popular saying “Innovate or die” is the driving force behind the startups that are springing up all over the world. However, these startups have quickly realized that innovation alone is not sufficient to sustain their business and continued development, and that is where we come in.

Startups require legal services to ease the company through registration and incorporation, and familiarize them with the legal and compliance terrain in which they seek to operate.

Brompton Solicitors has built her practice supporting startups, and we are known for offering affordable legal services to these new businesses.
